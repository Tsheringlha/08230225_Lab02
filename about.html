About Me

Hello! My name is Tshering Lhamo, born on May 3, 2004. I am a cheerful and curious person who enjoys learning new things every day. I love reading, drawing, and exploring creative activities that allow me to express myself. Music is also one of my passions, and I enjoy listening to songs that inspire and uplift my mood.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, sharing stories, and learning from their experiences. I like outdoor activities such as walking in nature, visiting new places, and experiencing different cultures. I also enjoy arts and crafts, painting, and creating simple DIY projects that allow me to explore my creativity.

I am a friendly and caring person who likes helping others and making people smile. I enjoy participating in group activities and working as a team, whether it’s for school projects, community events, or fun hobbies. Traveling, trying new foods, and learning about traditions are some of the things that excite me and broaden my perspective on life.

Overall, I am someone who loves to explore, learn, and enjoy life’s little moments. I value kindness, honesty, and positivity, and I always try to stay motivated and optimistic no matter the situation.
